### Branching Factor

![Branching Factor Graph]({{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/branching/Choices{{ page.title | replace:' ',''}}.png)

The figure above illustrates the branching factor (y-axis) for each decision point (x-axis) of {{ page.title }}, summarized over 100 random games. We define a decision point as a specific moment at which a player must choose from a set of possible actions. Following this definition, a player can be part of multiple distinct decision points during one of their turns, resulting in successive actions.
